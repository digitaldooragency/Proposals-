III. Digital Vision & Philosophy

The Philosophy: Authentic Dual-Channel Advocacy

We focus on Customer Advocacy across your primary revenue streams: In-Dining and Tiffin Service. Our content strategy shifts the focus from static food presentation to showcasing genuine diner experiences and the convenience of the Tiffin Box. We build brand loyalty and trust by highlighting these authentic voices in both contexts.

Strategic Objectives:

  • Expand Market Reach: Actively target and acquire new customers across digital channels.
  • Build the Foundation: Launch professional Instagram and Facebook pages immediately.
  • Amplify Voices: Utilize high-impact video testimonials and dynamic customer interviews to establish credibility and trust for both services.
  • Drive Footfall & Subscriptions: Leverage your existing Google Maps asset and targeted content to dominate local search results for both dining and delivery/tiffin.
